What we do

Transparent

Our mission is to empower customers and service workers by linking them directly. Finally you can tip someone and know exactly where the money is going.

Instant

Transactions within Tipease are instant from you to your server. From their account they can deposit payments within seconds. No more 1-3 day waiting periods you find on other applications.

Zero Fees

Tipsease is commited to benefit service workers of all types. That means zero fees, one hundred percent of the time.
